Addressing Customer Concerns and Objections

The R-A-C-E Framework — When a Customer Mentions a Competitor

When a customer brings up an independent shop or a competitor by name, R-A-C-E gives you a structure that respects their thinking and earns the right to make the Porsche case. Click each card to explore.

01
Respect
Acknowledge

  • Never badmouth or criticize
  • Sets a non-defensive tone
