What You'll Learn

By the end of this module, you will be able to do 5 things.

  • 01 Reframe customer objections as opportunities to educate and build trust
  • 02 Apply the CPR framework — Clarify, Paraphrase, Respond — when an objection is unclear
  • 03 Apply the R-A-C-E framework — Respect, Align, Contrast, Elevate — when a customer mentions a competitor
  • 04 Maintain professionalism when addressing customer concerns
  • 05 Recognize when to accept "no" gracefully while preserving the relationship
